✨Pronoun Stickers✨

Give your attendees a lovely way to communicate their pronouns

Why?

Some people use the pronouns she/her/hers or he/him/his and some use gender neutral pronouns such as they/them/theirs, etc. You can't always guess what pronouns someone uses just by looking at them, so having visible pronouns makes it easier for people to respectfully refer to each other. That’s why pronoun stickers are useful at events. It allows folks to specify their pronouns in a visible way so that we can all feel a little more comfortable together.

For further reading on this, see the Trans Pronoun FAQ.
